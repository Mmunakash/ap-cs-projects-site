Zombie Apocalypse

Its a zombie survival game where you are in the middle of a zombie chase! Run around using the WASD keys to not get caught and shoot the zombies using your mouse to kill the zombies and advance to the next round. As the round number increases, so does the difficulty. Pick up weapons that are on the ground using space bar to use different guns. GOOD LUCK.

Drawing App

This is my drawing app! The drawing app is an app where you can draw anything you want! I used snap to program the app. Press the green button to change your background color. CLick on the red button for the sprite to draw any shape you want. Click on the paint brush to free draw and click on the eraser to erase stuff that you drew. Finally, click on the yellow button to create points of your choice that will connect to draw a figure.
